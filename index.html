Selfly

your self-care App

Selfly is a single page web-application, exploring the concept of self-care.

The app will offers users easy options for self-care, depending on their mood and time they can spend on it. The idea was that one can pick how they feel in the moment from feelings ranging from happiness to sadness, throughout emotions such as frustration, anxiousness, stress, etc.

There are different activities that one can do for themselves to relieve those negative feelings and help themselves. And because we are living in a busy world it can be hard to find unlimited time to take care of oneself as well as finding the right activity can already take the precious time that we allocated for self-care.

The Space

Web-documentary

The Space is a web-documentary, created for an organisation located in Aarhus. The space is a place for internationals to meet, connect and grow together. Creating friendships and helping each other when coming to a new country - Denmark.

The Space has a strong storytelling connected with galactical travels as a way of telling the story of a person coming to a, for them, new planet. The concept of the web documentary is to tell a story of a new spacer coming to Denmark and finding The Space.

The way to explore the documentary is to scroll through the animations, going literally deeper and deeper into the story of a lonely Spacer finding their way into The Space.

Neptunvej

student housing application

Neptunvey is an application concept created for student housing located in Aarhus. The app is a functioning web-site to showcase the design and concept for the application.

The housing complex is located on a street called Naptunvey and therefore the design concept was to have a neptun theme where it would become the mascot of the app.

The application has a login in place as well as a booking for certain common areas such as the laundry room, workshop or party room. The front page would consist of the newest articles, current weather and info on upcoming holidays.

Freya

product showcase

Freya is a showcase website for leather bags. Freya is not a real product, the website was created for an exam assignment with a made-up scenario of working for a bag-making company to showcase their newest product.

Freya is a bag with a story, therefore the showcase is heavily focused on that aspect. The website also consists of a section about slow living, where one can read a little more about it and what it is.

The concept was to showcase the idea of lasting longer than products of other brands focusing on fast fashion and therefore supporting the idea of slow mindful living.

Crystal Ball

when you can't decide: yes or no

Crystal Ball is a small web-game made in my spare time. Do you ever have a problem deciding if you should have that one extra cup of coffee today? Me too! So now I don’t… I just blame it on the crystal ball ! Sorry I can’t go out tonight, the ball said no.

The Magical Crystal Ball deals with simple yes or no questions as it has a limited amount of answers. It is a one page website and the ‘game’ is controlled by a single button. The answer disappears after a few seconds and the ball is ready again!
